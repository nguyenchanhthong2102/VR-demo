Service: VR help relaxation

Comfort: Make sure your VR headset is comfortable to wear and fits properly. Adjust the straps and lenses as needed to avoid discomfort or eye strain.
Environment: Create a calming virtual environment that helps you relax. This could be a peaceful beach, a tranquil forest, or a soothing meditation room.

Sound: Use headphones to fully immerse yourself in the experience and block out external noise. Choose calming sounds, such as gentle waves or soft music, to further enhance relaxation.
Movement: Engage in slow and controlled movements to avoid motion sickness or dizziness. Take breaks and remove the headset if you start to feel uncomfortable.
Intention: Set an intention for your VR relaxation session. This could be to reduce stress, improve sleep, or simply to relax and unwind. Focusing on your intention can help you fully immerse yourself in the experience and achieve your desired outcome.
